# Chat Structure Rules

## Required sections

- Direct answer section heading.
- Steps section heading.
- Common mistakes section heading.

## Required formatting

- At least one bullet list in steps or mistakes.
- No single long paragraph output for informational answers.
- Keep short, kiosk-friendly lines.

## Language flexibility

- Heading labels can be localized in EN/AR/FR.
- Structure is required even when wording changes.
